ℹ️ How to read Half Balance▾ tap to expand

Half Balance compares your per-minute intensity across both halves using three metrics:

HSR per min40%High-speed running rate — most sensitive to fatigue
HID per min30%High-intensity distance rate — sustained hard efforts
Sprint dist per min30%Sprint output rate — explosive capacity

Using per-minute rates means subs and uneven playing time are handled fairly. The split (e.g. 55/45) shows the 1st-half share vs 2nd-half share of HSR output.

Categories:

✅ Consistent — Per-min intensity within 15% across halves. Ideal output.

🔸 Moderate fade — 10–15% per-min drop. Normal for most players.

⚠️ Faded — >15% per-min drop. Genuine fatigue flag for management.

🔵 Back-loaded — Intensity increased >15% in 2nd half. Not a concern — player was quieter early and ramped up.

🔄 SUB — Did not play both halves. Excluded from comparison.

Hover over the value in the table for the per-minute breakdown.
