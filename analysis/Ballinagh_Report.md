### Critical Issues

1. **1st-half kickout retention is catastrophic (49%)** — Ballinagh are effectively handing the opposition possession from every second restart in the opening period. This directly correlates with the large deficits built up in 1st halves across all three games.

3. **Free conversion at 57%** — below acceptable standard. With 14 frees across 3 games, the 6 missed frees represent approximately 6–8 points left behind (accounting for the 2-point free).

4. **Shooting from distance** — 42% of shots from play are taken from outside the 45m line with only a 31% conversion rate. The high "Short" count (8) confirms players are shooting beyond their effective range.
