🔍 Defensive Patterns

🎯 Force Poor Shooting (<40% Opp Accuracy)

The strongest defensive predictor. When opposition accuracy is held below 40%: 8W-1D-0L. When they shoot 40%+: 3W-0D-5L. Making them take difficult shots from distance is the best defence.
<40%: 8W-1D-0L≥40%: 3W-0D-5L

🟨 Points Conceded from Frees

When opposition score ≤3pts from frees: 9W-0D-4L. When they score 4+: 2W-1D-1L. The Arva draw (10pts from frees) and Denn Div 3 loss (10pts from frees) are the standout examples of fouling costing results.
≤3pts: 9W-0D-4L>3pts: 2W-1D-1L

🔒 Keep It Tight (≤12pts Conceded)

Unbeaten when holding opposition to 12 points or fewer: 5W-0D-0L. When conceding 13+: 6W-1D-5L. The 12-point mark is the defensive threshold.
≤12pts: 5W-0D-0L>12pts: 6W-1D-5L

🥅 Goals Conceded

0 goals conceded: 3W-0D-1L. 1 goal: 4W-1D-1L. 2+ goals: 4W-0D-3L. Goals conceded doesn't cleanly predict results — Killinkere have won games conceding 3 goals (v Drung, v Kill Shamrocks). But the Denn Div 3 loss (3 goals) shows it can be fatal against quality opposition.
0 goals: 3W-0D-1L1 goal: 4W-1D-1L2+ goals: 4W-0D-3L

🤐 Discipline (≤10 Frees Conceded)

Unbeaten when conceding 10 or fewer frees: 4W-0D-2L. When conceding 11+: 7W-1D-3L. Fewer fouls = fewer scoring chances for the opposition and less pressure on the defence.
≤10: 4W-0D-2L>10: 7W-1D-3L

📊 Where Conceded Scores Come From

Across 17 games: 76% of opposition scores come from play, 24% from frees. 58 points conceded from dead balls across the season — that's 22% of all points conceded.
