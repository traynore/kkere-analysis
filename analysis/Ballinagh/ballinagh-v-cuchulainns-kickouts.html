✅ Killinkere Game Plan — Kickout Pressure

  • Attack the 1st 15 minutes aggressively — Ballinagh's default opening strategy is to kick long into the middle third. They lost 6/8 of these in the 1st half. Set up a press in the 45m–65m zone and win the breaks.
  • Force them to keep going long — Their 2nd-half adjustment (going shorter) fixed the problem. If Killinkere push up and deny the short option, Ballinagh will be forced back into the long kickout strategy that failed catastrophically.
  • Win the break ball — "Break lost" (not "Lost clean") is the most common failure. Cuchulainns won by having a second man sweeping behind the contest. Station a runner behind your primary fielder.
  • Counter-attack immediately — Cuchulainns scored within 90 seconds of every turnover. Ballinagh's defence is disorganised in transition after losing their own restart. Go direct and fast.
  • Target their right side (your left) — Multiple kickouts landed in the Y: 0.05–0.25 zone (their right). This is where they lost most frequently. Overload that channel.
