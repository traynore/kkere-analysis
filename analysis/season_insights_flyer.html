✅ The Win Formula

When Killinkere win, these four conditions are consistently met:

  • ✅ Own kickout retention > 65%
  • ✅ Shooting accuracy > 50%
  • ✅ Possession lost < 16 per game
  • ✅ No scoreless spells > 10 minutes

🔑 Recommendations

Critical — Address Immediately

  • Shot selection: Too many low-percentage shots. Players with <45% accuracy need to work the ball to a better-placed teammate or take on a better angle. One extra pass before shooting could lift accuracy 10%+.
  • Ball retention under pressure: Kick pass turnovers spike in high-pressure games. Practice short recycling options when the primary kick pass is blocked.

Important — Tactical Focus

  • Protecting leads: Implement a game management plan when 4+ points up — slow the tempo, recycle possession, don't force shots. The team has lost leads of 6 points (Drumlane) and 5 points (Greenlough).
  • Second-half starts: Conceding goals in the first 5 minutes of 2nd halves is a recurring problem. Extra focus needed on defensive shape from throw-in.
  • Conall Mooney & Emmett Fitzsimons: Both taking high volumes of shots at very low conversion. Either work on finishing in training or reduce shot volume in favour of creating for others.
