ℹ️ How to read Half Balance▾ tap to expand

Half Balance compares your raw output across both halves using two metrics:

HSR (total metres)60%High-speed running — most sensitive to fatigue
Sprint distance (total metres)40%Sprint output — explosive capacity

Using raw totals means the comparison is based directly on GPS data with no estimated half durations. The split (e.g. 55/45) shows the 1st-half share vs 2nd-half share of weighted output.

Categories:

✅ Consistent — Output within 10% across halves. Ideal.

🔸 Moderate fade — 10–15% drop. Normal for most players.

⚠️ Faded — >15% drop. Genuine fatigue flag for management.

🔵 Back-loaded — Output increased >15% in 2nd half. Player was quieter early and ramped up.

🔄 SUB — Did not play both halves. Excluded from comparison.

Hover over the value in the table for the raw half breakdown.
